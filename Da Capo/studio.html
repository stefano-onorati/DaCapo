STUDIO INFO

Locations

The Toronto studio is located on Yonge Street (south of Eglinton Avenue and north of Davisville Avenue). It is minutes away from the Eglinton subway station and Davisville subway station.

The North York studio is located off of Sheppard Avenue West (east of Jane Street and west of Keele Street). The North York studio is just a short drive from the Downsview subway station.

Studios and Waiting Areas

Spacious and Fully Equipped Music Studios

Multiple Digital Pianos, Speakers, Mixers, Microphones, Computers, Variety of Instruments, Recording Devices, and Recording Software.

Both Studio Locations are kept clean and have air conditioning, windows for natural light, and free Wi-fi.

Both Studio Locations have a comfortable waiting area for students and parents.
The North York Studio has a television for waiting room viewing and free coffee.
